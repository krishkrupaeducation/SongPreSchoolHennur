We understand

Starting school is a big step — for your child and for you.

For many toddlers, preschool is their first experience away from home. Some children cry, some cling, some observe quietly before joining in. At S.O.N.G., we understand that this transition needs patience, warmth and trust.

Our toddler program is designed to help young children slowly settle into school life through play, routine, movement, music, stories and caring adult support.

Parent Questions

Questions parents usually ask before starting toddler school

What if my child cries?

That is completely normal. We support children gently and work with parents to make the transition smoother. Most children settle in well with patience and a consistent routine.

Does my child need to be toilet trained?

Not necessarily. We understand that toddlers are still developing independence and routines. Please speak with our team and we will guide you appropriately.

Is this too early for school?

The toddler program is not about pressure or formal academics. It is about comfort, confidence, social exposure and joyful early learning. It is a gentle, supportive start.

Can I visit before deciding?

Yes. Parents are welcome to book a school visit and meet the team. We encourage this — it helps you feel comfortable and ask everything you want to know.

What are the timings for the Toddler Program?

We offer Half Day (8:30 AM to 12:30 PM) and Full Day Care (8:30 AM to 6:00 PM). Our team will help you choose what works best for your child and family.

How do you ensure my toddler's safety?

We maintain a secure, child-friendly environment with trained staff, age-appropriate supervision and close parent communication at all times.
